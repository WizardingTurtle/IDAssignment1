UNENDING WAR

New America and Renvisioned Russia Fight an all out war over the fictional island of Orion in the year 2077

Weapons of war are at their peak, both sides' resources are at the brink of extinction and their political relations have reached the tipping point. The spark of war starts at a small island off the coast Renvisioned Russia as New America takes the initative to attack her opponent's critical oil reserves and precious metals needed to maintain Russia's war machine. Both sides have wronged each other, now its time to choose the lesser of evils in the start of this unending war

who will you side?
